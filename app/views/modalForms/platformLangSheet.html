Help Us Translate
If your language is not available or incomplete or inaccurate, please download the Webble World language files and help us translate the platform.
We use PoEdit for translations and if you want to join us in that work we recommend that you download the freeware version of PoEdit and use that when translating Webble World texts. PoEdit can be downloaded from here.
The current texts and language files are all conveniently zipped into a downloadable package that you can get from here.
When you are finished with a translation of a specific language, please e-mail the language file ([language id].po) to us and we will integrate it into the Webble World platform as soon as the translation as been validated.
The creator of every accepted translation will get his/her name added to the platform credits list in the 'About' section.
Thank you for your help!
